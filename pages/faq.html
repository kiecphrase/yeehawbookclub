How do I join?

If you see this, you're already qualified to be a member! Just read the book!

What do we do?

Read the book! And then write a review within the specified reading range :)

How do reviews work?

For all books not a multiple of 5, you should write a 1 page review. Pages should be 10 - 12 pt font, single spaced.

For all books that are a multiple of 5, you must write a 1 sentence review. 1 sentence only. No exceptions.

How do I submit my review?

Get it to the president of Yeehaw Book Club by the last day of the reading range! Any media or method is valid as long as the president gets it.
